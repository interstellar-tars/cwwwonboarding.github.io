Creating a Roblox Account

As part of your role, you will need a Roblox account. Please follow these steps to create one:

  1. Visit the Roblox Website:
    • Go to Roblox.com.
  2. Sign Up:
    • Click on the "Sign Up" button.
    • Fill in your birthday, username, password, and gender.
    • Username Requirement: Your username must start with either AMysteriousPerson if you work for AMysteriousPerson or StaryNight if you work for CodeWWW. For example, AMysteriousPerson123 or StaryNight456.
  3. Verify Your Account:
    • Complete any verification steps prompted by Roblox to secure your account.
  4. Confirm Your Email:
    • Check your email for a confirmation message from Roblox and follow the instructions to verify your email address.
  5. Set Up Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):
    • Go to your account settings.
    • Under the "Security" tab, enable two-factor authentication to add an extra layer of security.
  6. Customize Your Avatar (Optional):
    • Personalize your avatar as you see fit. This step is optional but can make your Roblox experience more enjoyable.
  7. Notify Your Supervisor:
    • Once your account is set up, inform your supervisor of your new username.

Your Role

As a Junior Research Employee, your primary responsibilities include:

  • Assisting senior researchers with ongoing projects.
  • Conducting literature reviews and summarizing findings.
  • Participating in brainstorming sessions and contributing ideas.
  • Assisting with data collection, analysis, and interpretation.
  • Preparing reports and presentations.

Key Skills and Competencies

  • Analytical thinking
  • Attention to detail
  • Strong written and verbal communication
  • Proficiency in research methodologies

First Day Checklist

  • Meet with HR: Complete all necessary paperwork and receive your employee badge.
  • IT Setup: Set up your workstation, including your computer, email, and access to necessary software.
  • Office Tour: Familiarize yourself with the office layout, including key areas like the kitchen, restrooms, and meeting rooms.
  • Meet Your Team: Introduce yourself to your team members and your mentor.
